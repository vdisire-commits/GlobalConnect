Frequently Asked Questions

Is GlobalConnect free to use? +
Absolutely! You can sign up, build your profile, connect with professionals, chat in real-time, and explore job opportunities‚Äîall for free. Our goal is to make professional networking easy and accessible for everyone.
How secure is my data on GlobalConnect? +
Your data security is our top priority. We use industry-standard encryption, secure authentication with JWT tokens, and follow GDPR compliance standards. All communications are encrypted end-to-end, and your personal information is never shared without your consent.
Can I post and apply for jobs on the platform? +
Absolutely! Both job seekers and employers can use GlobalConnect. Job seekers can browse opportunities, apply with cover letters, and save jobs for later. Employers can post job listings, track applicants, and find talent within your network.
How does the AI matching feature work? +
Our AI-ready matching system analyzes your skills, experience, and location to recommend jobs and connections that align with your profile. The smarter you make your profile, the better recommendations you'll receive.
Can I message people I'm not connected with? +
Currently, you can only message people you're connected with. This ensures a positive and focused networking environment. Send a connection request first, and once accepted, you can start chatting!
Is there a mobile app? +
GlobalConnect is fully responsive and works seamlessly on desktop, tablet, and mobile browsers. We're currently developing native mobile apps for iOS and Android to enhance your experience on the go.
